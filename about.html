The Artist

Meet
Prerna

Custom tattoo artist. Navi Mumbai. Turning stories into skin since the day ink stopped being a fear and became a calling.

Creative Artist

Specialising in visual storytelling — fusing beauty with purpose, crafting tattoos that deeply resonate.

Through thoughtful consultations, designs emerge as personalised reflections of transformation and resilience. Each tattoo embodies a unique narrative with emotional depth — not decoration, but identity made permanent.

Known to clients as Alza, Prerna brings a rare combination of creative direction, brand thinking, and studio precision to every session. The result: tattoos that feel inevitable, like they were always meant to be there.

The Journey

How a quiet child became
the artist behind the ink

01
Where Creativity Took Root

The Spark of Expression

A quiet child finds courage through movement, art, and performance. School events become the stage for exploring identity — transforming hesitation into self-expression and curiosity into skill.

Bravery Over Comfort

The Courage to Begin Again

Leaving home at 17, with dreams in one hand and uncertainty in the other. In a new city, everyday life becomes the first challenge. From surviving the pandemic alone to building inner strength, growth becomes a way of life.

03
Turning Scars into Stories

Art as Healing

What began as a fear of needles becomes a path to transformation. Tattooing evolves into a deeply personal practice — not just ink on skin, but symbols of strength, identity, and rebirth.

Designing Meaning, Leading with Purpose

The Storyteller's Mission

Years of creative direction, brand work, and studio operations shape a vision-driven artist. Every design now carries a story. Every session is a collaboration. The mission: create space for others to heal, express, and transform.
